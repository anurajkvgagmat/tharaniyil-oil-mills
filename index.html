Our Story

Tharaniyil Oil Mills began its journey in 1969, founded by T.V. Chacko with a simple yet powerful vision: to bring pure, natural coconut oil to every home. Rooted in the rich traditions of Kerala, our mill has grown from a small family operation into a trusted name known for quality, integrity, and authenticity. Every drop of oil we produce carries the warmth of our land and the dedication of three generations who have poured their heart into this craft. We remain committed to our origins — cold-pressing the finest coconuts to deliver oil that is as natural and wholesome today as it was over five decades ago.

Our Leaders

Leadership at Tharaniyil Oil Mill is built on quiet commitment and shared purpose. After founder T.V. Chacko, the mill is guided by his sons Raju Tharaniyil and Jolly Tharaniyil, whose steady leadership keeps the enterprise true to its roots while embracing steady growth. Shiny Raju continues to support the business as a partner, strengthening its foundation with care and consistency. Representing the third generation, Cyriac Tharaniyil is closely involved in day-to-day operations, driving sales and maintaining the quality and relationships that define Tharaniyil's reputation. The wider Tharaniyil family also contributes actively through branding, outreach, and marketing efforts, ensuring the legacy remains vibrant and relevant. Together, they lead with humility — guided by family, grounded in tradition, and united by trust.
