Our Story

Leadership at Tharaniyil Oil Mill is built on three generations of commitment to purity and excellence. Founded by late Appu Haji Tharaniyil and Asiyumma, our mill is guided by family values that prioritize quality above all.

We are committed to "Neetham", our core value, which represents honor, integrity, and respect in all that we do. This philosophy shapes every aspect of our operations.

The leadership combines traditional knowledge with modern techniques, ensuring that every bottle of Tharaniyil oil reflects our unwavering commitment to purity. Our business ethos is simple: produce the finest coconut oil while maintaining the trust our family has promised in markets and in our name.

Our Leadership

Under the guidance of our experienced leadership team, Tharaniyil Oil Mills continues to uphold the values established by our founders. Our leaders combine decades of industry expertise with a passion for delivering pure, natural products.

Each member of our leadership team is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality control and customer satisfaction. Their vision drives our commitment to innovation while preserving traditional extraction methods.

Together, they ensure that every bottle of Tharaniyil coconut oil meets our rigorous quality standards and carries forward our family's legacy of excellence.
